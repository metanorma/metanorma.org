== Document contributors

=== General

An IHO publication is typically developed under an IHO working group within
a committee.

The basic information of these groups are entered as document attributes,
including:

* the working group
* the committee

The `:committee:` and `:workgroup:` attributes are documented in the
link:/flavors/iho/ref/document-attributes[Metanorma IHO document attributes]
reference.
